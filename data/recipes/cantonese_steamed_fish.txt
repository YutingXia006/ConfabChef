Name: Cantonese Steamed Fish
Cuisine: Chinese (Cantonese)
Servings: 2
Calories: 280

Ingredients:
- 1 whole sea bass or tilapia (500g)
- 3 slices ginger, julienned
- 3 spring onions, julienned
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tsp sugar
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions:
1. Score fish on both sides and place on steaming plate.
2. Place ginger slices on top of fish.
3. Steam over high heat for 8-10 minutes until cooked through.
4. Mix soy sauce, oyster sauce and sugar in a bowl.
5. Pour sauce over fish.
6. Heat oil in pan until smoking, pour over fish.
7. Garnish with spring onions and coriander.
