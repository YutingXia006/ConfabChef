Name: Pak Choi with Shiitake Mushrooms
Cuisine: Chinese
Servings: 2
Calories: 180

Ingredients:
- 400g pak choi, halved
- 200g fresh shiitake mushrooms
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp cornstarch mixed with 3 tbsp water
- oil for frying

Instructions:
1. Blanch pak choi in boiling salted water for 1 minute, set aside.
2. Heat oil in wok over high heat.
3. Fry garlic until fragrant.
4. Add shiitake mushrooms and stir fry for 3 minutes.
5. Add oyster sauce and soy sauce.
6. Add cornstarch mixture and stir until sauce thickens.
7. Pour over pak choi and drizzle with sesame oil.
