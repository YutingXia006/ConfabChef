Name: Chinese Chicken Soup
Cuisine: Chinese
Servings: 4
Calories: 280

Ingredients:
- 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
- 3 slices ginger
- 3 cloves garlic
- 2 spring onions
- 1 tbsp rice wine
- salt to taste
- 2L water
- optional: red dates, wolfberries, goji berries

Instructions:
1. Blanch chicken in boiling water for 3 minutes, discard water.
2. Add chicken to fresh cold water and bring to boil.
3. Add ginger, garlic, spring onions and rice wine.
4. Simmer on low heat for 1.5 hours.
5. Add red dates and wolfberries in last 30 minutes.
6. Season with salt and serve.
