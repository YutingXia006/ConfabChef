Name: Mapo Tofu
Cuisine: Chinese (Sichuan)
Servings: 2
Calories: 320

Ingredients:
- 400g soft tofu, cubed
- 150g ground pork
- 2 tbsp doubanjiang (spicy bean paste)
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp ginger, minced
- 1 tsp Sichuan peppercorn
- 200ml chicken broth
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp cornstarch mixed with water
- spring onions for garnish

Instructions:
1. Fry Sichuan peppercorn in oil until fragrant, remove.
2. Fry doubanjiang, garlic and ginger until oil turns red.
3. Add ground pork and cook through.
4. Add broth and tofu, simmer 5 minutes.
5. Thicken with cornstarch mixture.
6. Garnish with spring onions and crushed peppercorn.
