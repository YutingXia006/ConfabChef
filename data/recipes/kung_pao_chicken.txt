Name: Kung Pao Chicken
Cuisine: Chinese
Servings: 2
Calories: 450

Ingredients:
- 300g chicken breast, diced
- 50g roasted peanuts
- 3 dried red chilies
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp ginger, minced
- 2 spring onions, chopped
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1 tsp cornstarch
- 1 tsp sugar
- oil for frying

Instructions:
1. Marinate chicken with soy sauce and cornstarch for 15 minutes.
2. Heat oil in wok over high heat.
3. Fry dried chilies and garlic until fragrant.
4. Add chicken and stir fry until golden.
5. Add peanuts, spring onions, vinegar, sugar and sesame oil.
6. Stir fry for 2 more minutes and serve with rice.

Tags: spicy, chinese, protein, quick
