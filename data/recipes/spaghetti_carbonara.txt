Name: Spaghetti Carbonara
Cuisine: Italian
Servings: 2
Calories: 620

Ingredients:
- 200g spaghetti
- 100g pancetta or bacon
- 2 eggs
- 50g parmesan, grated
- 2 cloves garlic
- black pepper
- salt

Instructions:
1. Cook spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
2. Fry pancetta with garlic until crispy.
3. Mix eggs and parmesan in a bowl.
4. Remove pan from heat, add drained pasta.
5. Add egg mixture quickly while tossing.
6. Season with black pepper and serve immediately.
