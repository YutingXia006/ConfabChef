Name: Chinese Fried Rice
Cuisine: Chinese
Servings: 2
Calories: 380

Ingredients:
- 2 cups cooked rice (day old is best)
- 2 eggs
- 100g frozen peas and carrots
- 3 spring onions, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- oil for frying

Instructions:
1. Heat wok on high heat until smoking.
2. Add oil and scramble eggs, set aside.
3. Fry garlic until fragrant.
4. Add rice and stir fry breaking up clumps.
5. Add vegetables and soy sauce.
6. Add eggs back, sesame oil and spring onions.
7. Serve immediately.
